### DECISION POLICY  
Compute: `temp_gap_f = temp_gap(forecast_peak_temp_f, max_allowable_temp_f)`  
- **AT_RISK** → gap ≥ 20 °F  
- **BORDERLINE** → 10–19 °F  
- **NOT_AT_RISK** → < 10 °F  
- **INSUFFICIENT_DATA** → any key value missing
